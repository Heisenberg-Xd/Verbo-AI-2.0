The rise of streaming platforms like Twitch and YouTube Gaming has also contributed to the popularity of gaming, as millions of viewers tune in to watch their favorite streamers and content creators play games, offer commentary, or compete in tournaments. Virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) gaming are also on the rise, creating immersive experiences that push the boundaries of what games can offer. The rise of professional gaming, or esports, has further elevated gaming's status.
