The FIFA Fußball-Weltmeisterschaft 2022 in Qatar was a historic tournament full of dramatic moments and unforgettable games. Lionel Messi led Argentina to the third World Championship title in a Final against France, which many experts described as the best World Cup Final of all time. The game ended 3-3 after extra time, with Messi scoring two goals before Argentina won the shootout. For Messi, who has long stood in the shadow of Diego Maradona, this Triumph was the crowning of an extraordinary career. Kylian Mbappe scored a hat-trick in the Final and brought France back to 3-2 after a 0-2 deficit. Despite this incredible Einzelleistung, France could not prevent the Argentine victory. Mbappe plays for Paris Saint-Germain in the French Ligue 1 and is regarded as the future dominant player of the world football.
