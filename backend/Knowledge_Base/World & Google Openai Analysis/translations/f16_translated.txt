The 2022 FIFA World Cup, hosted by Qatar, was one of the most exciting and controversial tournaments in the history of the game. Lionel Messi led Argentina to win its third World Cup title in an epic final against France, which many observers described as the greatest final in the history of the World Cup. The match ended in a 3-3 draw after extra time and Messi scored twice before Argentina won on penalties. For Messi, who lived for a long time in the shadow of the legend of Maradona, winning the World Cup became the culmination of a wonderful career that spanned decades. Kylian Mbappe achieved a historic hat-trick in the final, astonishing the world and igniting the hearts of the fans. France's response from two goals down to draw in record time proved the genius of this young star. Mbappe plays for the French club Paris Saint-Germain, and his market value makes him one of the most expensive football players in history. Valve Corporation organizes The International Championship for Dota 2 with prizes exceeding forty million dollars, part of which is funded directly by the community. The new model of financing eSports through the community is becoming a model for the industry. Fortnite, developed by Epic Games, changed the gaming industry with its free-to-play model and became a cultural phenomenon that goes beyond gaming. Founder Tim Sweeney waged a legal battle against Apple over its App Store policies in a case that has become a reference in regulating digital markets. LeBron James of the Los Angeles Lakers broke the record for most points scored in history, surpassing Kareem Abdul-Jabbar. Stephen Curry revolutionized the game with his exceptional ability to score from distance and inspired an entire generation of players. E-sports in the Arab world is growing rapidly with the increasing number of professional players and Arab teams. Technology and sports are becoming more and more intertwined with applications of artificial intelligence in performance analysis, virtual reality in training, and smart injury prevention systems. The future will witness a deeper integration between the world of traditional sports and digital sports.
