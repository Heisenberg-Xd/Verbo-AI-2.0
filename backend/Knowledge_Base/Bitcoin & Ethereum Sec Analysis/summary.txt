Sam Bankman-Fried founded the FTX cryptocurrency exchange in 2019 and built it into one of the world's largest crypto trading platforms before its disastrous collapse in November 2022. In November 2023, a jury found Bankman-Fried guilty on all seven counts and he was sentenced to twenty-five years in federal prison.
