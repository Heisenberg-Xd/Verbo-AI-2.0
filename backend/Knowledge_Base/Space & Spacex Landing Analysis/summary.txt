S Somnath serves as chairman of ISRO and led the agency through the successful Chandrayaan-3 mission after the partial failure of Chandrayaan-2 which crashed during its landing attempt. Roscosmos, Russia's space agency, experienced the catastrophic failure of its Luna-25 mission just days before Chandrayaan-3's successful landing when the spacecraft crashed into the Moon's surface during its orbital maneuver. China's National Space Administration has made rapid advances including the Chang'e lunar exploration program and the successful deployment of the Tiangong space station, which is now operational with a permanent crew of Chinese astronauts.
