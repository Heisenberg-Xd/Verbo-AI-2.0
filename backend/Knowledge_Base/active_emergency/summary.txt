The Emergency Fund: Why Most Financial Planners Agree This Is Step One

Before discussing investment returns, tax optimization, or retirement accounts, virtually every credentialed financial planner will ask the same first question: do you have an emergency fund? At 5 percent, a $15,000 emergency fund generates $750 per year in risk-free interest while remaining instantly accessible. Active funds typically charge 0.5 percent to 1.5 percent, with many hedge funds adding a two-and-twenty structure — two percent of assets under management plus twenty percent of profits.
