    Cryptocurrency Trading - Hindi

1. Cryptocurrency trading is the process of buying and selling digital currencies. 2. Bitcoin is the world's first and most popular cryptocurrency. 3. Ethereum (ETH) is a programmable blockchain that runs smart contracts. 4. The crypto market is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week, unlike the traditional stock market. 5. In Spot Trading you buy and sell real crypto. 6. In Futures Trading you enter into a contract to buy or sell crypto at a future price. 7. Altcoins like Solana, Cardano, and Polkadot are alternatives to Bitcoin. 8. Market Capitalization reflects the total market value of a crypto. 9. HODL is a popular crypto term that means holding for a long time. 10. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) forces traders to take hasty decisions. 11. FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt) spreads negative sentiment in the market. 12. In Technical Analysis the price is estimated using charts and patterns. 13. In Fundamental Analysis the technology and team of the project are evaluated. 14. Binance, WazirX, and CoinDCX are popular crypto exchanges in India. 15. DeFi (Decentralized Finance) provides financial services without banks. 16. In Yield Farming you lend your crypto for interest. 17. NFT (Non-Fungible Token) represents a unique digital asset. 18. Always use trusted platforms to avoid Crypto Scams. 19. In India, 30% tax has to be paid on crypto profits. 20. Never share Private Key with anyone, it is the security of your property. 21. Hardware Wallets like Ledger and Trezor are the most secure storage options. 22. Bitcoin Halving happens every four years which cuts its supply in half. 23. Stablecoins like USDT are used to stay safe in a volatile market. 24. When investing in crypto, only invest money you can afford to lose.
