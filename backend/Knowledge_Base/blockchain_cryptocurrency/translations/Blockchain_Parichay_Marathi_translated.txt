=======================================
       Blockchain - A Complete Introduction
       Blockchain - Sampurna Parichay
=======================================

1. Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions. 2. It stores data in "blocks" and these blocks are interconnected. 3. Once data is recorded in the blockchain, it is nearly impossible to change or delete it. 4. Bitcoin was the first successful blockchain application, launched in 2009. 5. Blockchain technology is believed to have been developed by Satoshi Nakamoto. 6. Each block contains transaction data, timestamp and hash of previous block. 7. Hash is a unique digital fingerprint that gives a unique identity to each block. 8. Decentralization means no single organization has control. 9. Consensus mechanism creates agreement among all network nodes. 10. In Proof of Work, miners solve complex mathematical problems. 11. Validators in Proof of Stake are selected based on their holdings. 12. Smart Contracts are self-executing programs that run on the blockchain. 13. Ethereum is the most popular blockchain for smart contracts. 14. Public blockchain is open to all, while private blockchain is only for authorized users. 15. Hyperledger Fabric is an enterprise-grade private blockchain framework. 16. Blockchain brings transparency to supply chain management. 17. Blockchain is used in Healthcare to secure patient records. 18. Blockchain can help prevent fraud in voting systems. 19. DeFi (Decentralized Finance) provides financial services based on blockchain. 20. NFT (Non-Fungible Token) proves unique digital ownership on the blockchain. 21. Web3 is the future of the internet based on blockchain technology. 22. Gas fees are fees to be paid for transactions on the blockchain. 23. Layer 2 solutions solve the problems of scalability and speed of blockchain. 24. Blockchain technology will change banking, governance and identity management in the future.
