The French music industry between cultural heritage and digital revolution

France occupies a unique position in the global musical landscape, at the intersection of centuries-old artistic traditions and a creative industry fully engaged in digital transformation. The emergence of contemporary artists such as Stromae, Angèle, and Aya Nakamura has demonstrated that music that is resolutely French in its language and influences can conquer international markets. French cultural policy plays a structuring role in the national music industry.
