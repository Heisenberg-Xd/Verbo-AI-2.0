Unlike public market investors who face daily mark-to-market losses, venture funds are only required to mark down private portfolio companies when there is a "significant observable transaction" suggesting a lower value. OpenAI closed a $10 billion investment from Microsoft in early 2023, valuing the company at roughly $29 billion. Buying ten percent of a pre-revenue company at a $10 million valuation requires only a $100 million outcome — a threshold that the market can absorb even when unicorn fantasies are out of fashion.
