OYO, the Indian hospitality company, went public at a significantly reduced valuation after Vision Fund had invested at a $10 billion peak. Unlike public market investors who face daily mark-to-market losses, venture funds are only required to mark down private portfolio companies when there is a "significant observable transaction" suggesting a lower value. OpenAI closed a $10 billion investment from Microsoft in early 2023, valuing the company at roughly $29 billion.
