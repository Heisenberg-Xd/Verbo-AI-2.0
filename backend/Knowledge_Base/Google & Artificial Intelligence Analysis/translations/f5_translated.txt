Artificial intelligence has become one of the most important areas in modern technology. The organization OpenAI has developed GPT-4, a highly advanced language model that is used by millions of people around the world. Led by Sam Altman, OpenAI launched ChatGPT which reached ten million users in just two months. This technology is revolutionizing education, health and industry. Google has teamed up with DeepMind to create Gemini, a multidimensional AI model. DeepMind has succeeded in accurately predicting protein structure through the AlphaFold project, which is crucial for drug research. Sundar Pichai has called Gemini the most important project in Google's history. The competition between Google and Microsoft is giving a big boost to the development of AI technology. Microsoft has invested heavily in OpenAI and has included GPT-4 in Bing and Azure. Satya Nadella has called artificial intelligence the most important technology of the future. Jensen Huang of NVIDIA said that today we stand on the threshold of a new industrial revolution. NVIDIA's H100 GPU chips are used worldwide for training AI models. Anthropic, a company founded by Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei, developed the secure AI model Claude. Google has invested heavily in this company. Meta has released LLaMA, an open source language model that is freely available to researchers. This open source model has made it possible for even small companies and researchers to use AI technology. The use of AI technology is growing rapidly in Maharashtra. Many startup companies in Pune and Mumbai are developing AI based products. Researchers from IIT Mumbai and IIT Pune are making important contributions in the field of AI. Big IT companies like TCS, Infosys and Wipro are leaders in providing AI solutions. Appropriate and responsible use of artificial intelligence is one of the biggest challenges of today. Ethical AI development requires international cooperation.
