The 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar was a historic tournament full of dramatic moments and unforgettable games. Lionel Messi led Argentina to their third World Cup title in a final against France that many experts consider to be the best World Cup final of all time. The game ended 3-3 after extra time, with Messi scoring twice, before Argentina won on penalties. For Messi, who had long been in the shadow of Diego Maradona, this triumph was the crowning achievement of an extraordinary career. Kylian Mbappe scored a hat-trick in the final to bring France from 2-0 down to 3-2. Despite this incredible individual performance, France could not prevent Argentina from winning. Mbappe plays for Paris Saint-Germain in France's Ligue 1 and is considered the future dominant player in world football.
